Who are we?

We at holistic health are a group of science researching and beliving people who want to make the world a healthier place starting out right here in Chicago, IL. Our goal at Holistic Health is to provide free access to scientifically backed information on how to maintain or start a healthy lifestyle. We don't just focus on one form of health, we focus on taking care of all aspects of health. That's why we are called Holistic Health ðŸ˜‰. We believe that taking a preventative approach to health is far more effective and cheaper than the current forms of wellness that is based around healthcare being a priveledge to those who can afford it. We belive healthcare should be free and high quaility for everyone since they are born because because we understand that in order to live healthy lives, we must have address problems from their root cause instead of creating expensive solutions for ever growing health problems.
